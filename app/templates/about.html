About KidzAgent

Our Mission

Voice Assisted KidzAgent is a privacy-first, voice-based "reading buddy" designed for kids ages 5–8 to practice reading aloud at home in short, parent-nearby sessions. We believe that learning should be safe, supportive, and effective without compromising privacy.

How It Works

  1. Start a Session: Choose a reading passage from our carefully curated collection.
  2. Read Aloud: Read the passage while KidzAgent listens in real time.
  3. Get Feedback: Receive gentle corrections and explanations for any words you stumble on.
  4. Build Confidence: Earn encouraging messages and track your progress over time.

Why KidzAgent?

  • Safety First: Built to provide kid-safe responses by default. No ads, no data collection.
  • Effective Learning: Focuses on real outcomes: decoding, pronunciation, and comprehension.
  • Parent-Friendly: Works best with a parent nearby. Includes a parental dashboard to track progress.
  • Low Friction: Short sessions (5-15 minutes) fit easily into busy family schedules.

Privacy & Safety

KidzAgent is designed with children's privacy and safety as core principles:

  • No personal data collection beyond what's needed for reading sessions
  • All AI responses are filtered for age-appropriateness
  • Audio is processed securely and not stored permanently
  • Parental controls ensure appropriate content

Expected Impact

  • More frequent, low-friction reading practice for early readers
  • Reduced stress for families trying to fit reading support into busy schedules
  • A clear example of how to design child-friendly, privacy-first voice AI
